What you should know about us

1. It's easy to use our service

Not to say that the current method of collecting donations is broken, we just think that it could use some work. We believe that the charity world is not taking full advantage of the direction modern transactions are moving and we try to fix that. Every charity in one place, ultra direct donation system, and no hassle donations: that is our mission.

2. We're completely non profit

We don't take anything off the top or throw up unsightly advertisements. Just a self-funded, self-managed, give-back-to-the-world website.

3. We are a new way to donate

These days you have to fill out long web forms or take advantage of snail mail to donate to charities. We think that leaves them at a disadvantage - everybody is busy and sometimes can't spare the time to go through this process. When is the last time you helped the world at the click of a button?
